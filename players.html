Players

This page shows all the players on the current roster. Each player card displays their name and photo, with a maximum of 12 players allowed.

Click View More to see their stats, edit their photo, or remove them from the roster.

Click + Add New Player to add a new player to the roster. Enter their stats on a scale of one to ten, which the system will use to automatically calculate their play style.

In the case a player is accidentally added or removed, click Undo ↺ to undo the action.
